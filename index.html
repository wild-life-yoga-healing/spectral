About

Jayna Staykov is an Australian-born yoga teacher, now living in Shanghai. It all started with a desire to touch her toes; soon enough she fell in love with the healing qualities of yoga and the beautiful connection between mind, body and breath. Her classes are centered on creating meditation in movement, using asana to journey inwards and explore what is there within us. She encourages using the practice of yoga to connect deeply within our own hearts, to open it up to others and to cultivate a connection to the earth bringing us back to our true wild nature. Jayna also has an undergraduate degree in psychology and where possible incorporates this knowledge into her teachings.
