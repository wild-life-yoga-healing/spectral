Jayna is an Australian-born yoga teacher now living in Shanghai. It all started with an ambition to touch her toes soon enough she fell in love with the healing qualities of yoga and the beautiful connection between mind, body and breath. Her classes are centered on creating meditation in movement through vinyasa flow, using asana to journey inwards and explore what is there within us. She encourages using the practice of yoga to connect deeply within our own hearts, to open it up to others and to cultivate a connection to the earth bringing us back to our true nature. Jayna also has an undergraduate degree in psychology and where possible incorporates this knowledge into her teachings.

Qualifications

  • Registered Yoga Teacher Certification (RYT), since 2015
  • Foundation Yoga Teacher Training 200hr Certification at BodyMindLife, Sydney
  • Senior Teachers: Simon Park, Noelle Connolly and Kat Clayton
  • Graduate Diploma in Psychology at University of New England
  • Reiki Level 1 Healer
